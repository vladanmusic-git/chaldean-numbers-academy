Privacy Policy

Effective date: 9 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what personal data the Chaldean Numbers Academy app collects, why we collect it, who processes it on our behalf, and the choices you have. We keep data collection to the minimum needed to run the app.

1. Who we are

The data controller is CP Global Dynamics LLC, a company organized under the laws of the State of California, United States (owner: Vladan Mijatović), 425 North Doheny Drive, Unit 2, Beverly Hills, CA 90210, USA. For any privacy request, contact us at cipijazzai@gmail.com. We have not appointed a separate Data Protection Officer; requests are handled at the same address.

3. Service providers (processors)

We use a small set of trusted providers to run the app. They process data only to provide their service to us:

  • Supabase — account authentication and secure storage of your account data and progress.
  • RevenueCat — subscription and purchase management.
  • OpenAI and Google (Gemini) — generate AI study-tutor answers from the text you send (see section 4).
  • PostHog — anonymous product-usage analytics to improve the app.
  • Sentry — crash and performance diagnostics.

Apple processes your subscription payments; we never see your full payment-card details.

4. The AI study tutor

The AI tutor answers questions about the course material. When you use it, the text of your message — and the name and birth date you entered for calculations if your question refers to them — is sent through our own backend to OpenAI and Google (Gemini) to generate a reply. This happens only after you grant explicit in-app consent, which is shown before your first message. Your chat content is not used for advertising and is not sold. No other data is shared with these AI providers.

5. Advertising, tracking, cookies and local storage

We do not use third-party advertising, we do not track you across other companies’ apps or websites, and we do not sell or share your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

The app does not use cookies. Your profile, progress, and settings are kept in the device’s local storage and can be removed by deleting your account or the app. These informational web pages set no cookies either.

6. How long we keep data

We keep your account data and progress while your account is active. If you delete your account, we delete the associated personal data from our systems (see section 8). Anonymous, aggregated usage and diagnostic data may be retained to keep the app reliable. Providers may retain data for short periods as required for security and legal compliance.

7. Your rights

If you are in the EU/EEA, the UK, or another jurisdiction with similar data-protection law (including Serbia), you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data;
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data;
  • Erasure — have your data deleted (see section 8 for the in-app path);
  • Restriction — limit how we process your data;
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interest;
  • Data portability — receive the data you provided in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format;
  • Withdraw consent — at any time, where processing is based on consent (for example the AI tutor), without affecting processing that happened before withdrawal;
  • Complain — lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority (in Serbia, the Commissioner for Information of Public Importance and Personal Data Protection).

California residents (CCPA/CPRA): you have the right to know what personal information we collect, to request access, deletion, and correction, and to opt out of “sale” or “sharing”. We do not sell personal information and do not share it for cross-context behavioral advertising, and we do not discriminate against you for exercising any of these rights. Authorized agents may submit requests on your behalf.

To make any request, email cipijazzai@gmail.com. We respond within the timelines required by the applicable law (typically 30–45 days).

Data breaches: if a personal-data breach is likely to put your rights at risk, we will notify the competent authority without undue delay (where feasible within 72 hours of becoming aware) and inform affected users where the breach is likely to result in a high risk.

8. Deleting your account

You can delete your account and its data at any time from inside the app: open “Više” (More / Settings) → Delete account. Deletion is immediate. You can also request deletion by email. See Delete Your Account for details.

9. Children

The app is rated 4+ and is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

10. International transfers

Our providers may process data on servers in the United States and other countries. Where required, transfers rely on appropriate safeguards such as the providers’ standard contractual clauses.

11. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we will update this page and revise the effective date above.
