Managing your subscription

Subscriptions are billed through your Apple App Store account. To view, change, or cancel a subscription, open the Settings app on your iPhone → tap your name → Subscriptions → select Chaldean Numbers Academy. Cancelling stops the next renewal; you keep access until the end of the paid period.

Restoring purchases

If you reinstall the app or switch devices, open the app’s subscription screen and tap Restore Purchases to re-link an active subscription to your Apple ID.

Free trial

Both subscriptions include a 7-day free trial. You are not charged during the trial and can cancel any time before it ends from your App Store subscription settings.

Deleting app data

You can delete the anonymous technical account and all app data at any time — see Delete App Data.
