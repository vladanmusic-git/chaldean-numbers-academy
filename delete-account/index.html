Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your account and its personal data at any time, directly inside the app.

In the app (immediate)

  1. Open Chaldean Numbers Academy.
  2. Go to “Više” (More / Settings) — the last tab.
  3. Tap Delete account and confirm.

Your account and the personal data associated with it (name, email, birth-date input, learning progress) are deleted from our systems immediately. This cannot be undone.

By email

Prefer email? Write to cipijazzai@gmail.com from the address on your account and we will delete it within 24 hours.

A note on subscriptions

Deleting your account does not cancel an Apple subscription. Cancel it separately in the iPhone Settings app → your name → Subscriptions, so it does not renew.
