Delete App Data

Version 1 offers optional Sign in with Apple and Continue as Guest. You can permanently delete either kind of app account together with all app data directly inside the app.

In the app (immediate)

  1. Open Chaldean Numbers Academy.
  2. Go to “Više” (More / Settings) — the last tab.
  3. Tap “Trajno obriši moje podatke” (Permanently delete my data) and confirm.

The Apple-linked or anonymous guest app account is deleted from our systems, and local calculator data, profile values, chat history, purchase history, and learning progress are removed from the device immediately. This cannot be undone.

By email

Need help? Write to cipijazzai@gmail.com and we will respond within 24 hours. The in-app action is the immediate deletion method for both Apple-linked and guest accounts.

A note on subscriptions

Deleting app data does not cancel an Apple subscription. Cancel it separately in the iPhone Settings app → your name → Subscriptions, so it does not renew.
